Introduction

Hello! I'm Leonardo, a passionate Site Reliability Engineer with a love for creating robust systems and ensuring they run smoothly. My journey in technology started when I was young through programming challenges, which sparked my interest in problem-solving through code.

I initially tried my hand at game development but never quite got the hang of it. During my first year of engineering degree at IME-USP, I landed a Data Scientist Intern position at Loft. From there, I evolved through different roles - from Data Science to MLOps, and finally found my calling in the SRE world, where I get to combine my love for coding with systems reliability.

When I'm not ensuring systems stay up and running, you can find me playing baseball, watching football (go Palmeiras!), or diving into a good game. I'm currently pursuing my Computer Science degree at IME-USP while working full-time.
